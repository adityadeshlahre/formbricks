#### Add Image or Video to Question

# Image or Video

You can add image or videos to any question to provide clearer instructions for respondents. This enhances clarity and makes the question more engaging and interactive, creating a better survey experience.

## Elements

By clicking on this option you can enable Image and video addition to your question.
